FreeState Solar develops permitted, interconnection-approved community solar projects in National Grid territory — ready for capital partners who want direct access to the New York CDG market.

New York CDG

All projects are structured under New York's Value of Distributed Energy Resources (VDER) tariff, providing long-term, state-backed revenue certainty.

—

5 MW-AC Ground Mount

Projects are optimized for the NY CDG program cap — single-axis tracker, ground-mount, in National Grid Zone F territory with cleared interconnection capacity.
