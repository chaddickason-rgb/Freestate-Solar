Our Company

Two decades of solar.
One region.

FreeState Solar is a community solar developer based in Tivoli, New York. We have been working in the solar industry for over twenty years and have been developing projects specifically in New York for more than a decade.

Experience that matters

Our team's experience spans the full project lifecycle — from initial site screening and interconnection analysis through permitting, financing, procurement, and construction management. We have worked on solar farms ranging from a few acres to well over 100 acres across New York and the Northeast.

We have spent more than ten years navigating New York's regulatory environment — including NYISO interconnection, Article 10 and local permitting, VDER tariff structures, CDG subscription management, and the evolving community solar policy landscape.

Where we work

FreeState focuses exclusively on the Hudson Valley and Upstate New York. Our primary territory is National Grid's Zone F — Columbia, Schoharie, Washington, Fulton, Otsego, Warren, and Saratoga Counties. We also evaluate projects in Central Hudson, NYSEG, Con Edison, and Orange & Rockland territories where conditions warrant.

This geographic focus is not a limitation — it is a deliberate strategy. We know the local permitting bodies, the grid infrastructure, the land market, and the communities. That knowledge compresses timelines and reduces project risk.

How we work

FreeState operates as a lean developer — we do not build to hold. We bring projects from greenfield through NTP and work with capital partners and tax equity investors to bring them to commercial operation. Our deal structures are designed to align interests across landowners, investors, and the communities that receive clean energy benefits.

We take a direct, straightforward approach to every relationship. No intermediaries, no excess overhead. You deal with the people who made the decisions.
